# Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Can students from other universities join?
Currently, ProjectHub is pre-configured specifically for Kalasalingam University students. It requires university verification schemas to participate in collaborative teams.

### 2. Can I browse projects without logging in?
Yes! The platform features a built-in **Demo Mode** which allows recruiters and guests to browse the dashboard, explore sample active workspaces, and read student profiles without creating an account.

### 3. How does the Collaboration Score get calculated?
The Collaboration Score is the average rating given by your teammates upon completion of joint projects. It serves as a decentralized peer recommendation index.
